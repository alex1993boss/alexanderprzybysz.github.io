This advanced device is specifically designed for plant phenotyping, focusing on the detailed study of plant roots for research purposes. It captures high-resolution images of seeds and their roots throughout the germination cycle. These images are then processed by a sophisticated machine learning model that analyzes various factors, including root growth speed, growth patterns, germination success rates, and more. In addition to its imaging capabilities, the device automates key environmental conditions such as light and growth medium temperature, creating an optimal environment for seed germination and root development.

  • Electronic Hardware Development: Designing and assembling the electronic components that form the backbone of the device.
  • Firmware Development: Creating robust firmware to ensure seamless operation and integration of all hardware components.
  • Control and Scheduling Software: Developing sophisticated software for precise actuation, light control, and temperature regulation, ensuring the device operates autonomously and efficiently.
