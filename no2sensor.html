This innovative prototype is a self-contained environmental sensor designed to detect Nitrogen Dioxide (NO2) gas with high precision. The device operates by pumping ambient air into a specialized chamber housing an indium gallium zinc oxide-based transducer, which is highly sensitive to Nitrogen Dioxide. This allows for accurate monitoring of NO2 levels in various environments, making it an essential tool for environmental monitoring and research.

  • Enclosure Design and Fabrication: Creating a durable and functional enclosure to protect the internal components and ensure reliable operation in various environmental conditions.
  • Circuitry Design and Fabrication: Engineering the electronic circuitry to support the transducer and overall functionality of the device.
  • Component Integration: Seamlessly integrating all components to ensure cohesive and efficient operation of the sensor.
